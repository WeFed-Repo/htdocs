Records

Permits the user to view the records of all subjects with whomthe company has business relationships; Clients, Suppliers, Employees.

The records are particularly important during the preparation of instructions, as it is possible to look up a record and transfer all the necessary information into the instruction.

It is possible to enter and manage records for the following:

  • Clients

  • Suppliers

  • Foreign Suppliers (Optional)

  • Employees

  • ForeignCreditors (Optional)

  • ForeignDebtors (Optional)


It is also possible to carry out:

  • ImportRecords(Optional)

  • CancelRecords (Optional)
