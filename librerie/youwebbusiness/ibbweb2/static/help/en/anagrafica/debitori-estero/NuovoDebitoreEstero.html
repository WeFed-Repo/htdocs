New ForeignDebtor

Permits the creation of a record for a new foreign debtor.
The entry interface consists of two sections:

  • ForeignDebtor Data
  • Bank Data

Company Name/Account Holder: select the name of the company for which to enter a new foreign debtor record

Foreign DebtorData Section
The following fields are present:

  • Debtor code: indicate the code with which to identify the foreign debtor
  • Company Name: indicate the name of the foreign debtor
  • Siren/Siret Code: indicate the fiscal code or VAT number of the debtor. In the case of Spain being indicated in the country field it must be left empty; in the case of France being indicated and the amount is 50,000 Euro or higher then the field must be filled in with nine figures.
  • Address: indicate the foreign debtor's address
  • Post Code: indicate the Post Code of the foreign debtor
  • Town: indicate the town of residence of the foreign debtor
  • Prov.: indicate the province of the foreign debtor
  • Country : select the country of the foreign debtor

Bank Data Section
The following fields are present:

  • National Bank Coordinates: indicate the national bank coordinates of the foreign debtor's account. With the Help national Bank Coordinates it is possible to see how they are structured.
  • Account: indicate the current account number of the foreign debtor
  • CIN: indicate the checkkey of the bankcoordinates of the foreigndebtor's account
  • IBAN: indicate the international coordinates of the foreign debtor's account. With the Help IBAN button it is possible to see how it is structured.
  • Bank-Branchdescription

Company Name/Account Holder: select the name of the company for which to enter a new foreign debtor record.

Click on Enter to confirm the data entered.

Click on Cancel to cancel the operation.
