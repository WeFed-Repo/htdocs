InstructionDetail

Presents detailed information of the collection instruction selected.

The page is divided in two sections:

  • Instruction detail
  • List of messages regarding advancement status

Instruction detail

The sectionisdividedintosix sub-sections:

OrdererData :groups together the information regarding the orderer of the instruction

Effective Creditor Data: indicates the name of the creditor in the case of it being different to the orderer

Debtor Data: groupstogether the information regarding the instructiondebtor

Effective Debtor Data: indicates the name of the effective debtor in the case of it being different to the specified debtor

Instruction Data: groups together the specific information regarding the instruction:
The main fields present are:

  • Type of Collection: indicates the type of collection
  • Amount
  • Send date: indicates the date in which the collectioninstructionwassent
  • Expiry date: indicates the date of expiry of the collection specified in the instruction
  • Collection description: indicates the uniqueidentificationnumber of the collectionpresent in the instruction
  • Collection sequence: indicates the sequence of the collectionpresent in the instruction
  • Match information: indicates the match information specified in the instruction

Extra Information : groups together extra information regarding the instruction being consulted.
The main fields present are:

  • Mandate Code: indicates the identificationnumber of the mandate signed by the debtor
  • Date of subscription: indicates the date the mandate was signed
  • Status: indicates the result of the instruction. It can be
    • Signed
    • Confirmed: the paymentrequesthasbeenaccepted by the Bank
    • Rejected:the paymentrequesthasbeenrefused due to formalreasons or substantialcontrols.

List of messages regarding advancement status

If foreseen by the type of collection this section is displayed only if there are results present or messages regarding the advancement status.

The information shown is structured as follows:

  • Creation date: indicates the creation date of the result/advancement status message
  • Upload date: indicates the date which the result/ advancement status messagewas made available
  • Status: indicates the result of the instruction/advancement status message. It can be
    • Confirmed: the payment request has been accepted by the Bank
    • Rejected:the payment request has been refused due to formal reasons or substantial controls
  • Status description
